# Aether — AI Wingman

Control your Mac from your iPhone with natural language.

Aether is a macOS menu-bar app that pairs with an iOS companion. Type or speak
a command on your phone — "open Spotify and play my focus playlist" — and your
Mac does it.

> **Status:** Alpha. Signed + notarized builds published here. iOS app on the App Store (pending review).

---

## Install

1. Download the latest `Aether-<version>.dmg` from the [Releases](../../releases) page.
2. Open the DMG and drag **Aether** into Applications.
3. Launch Aether from Spotlight or Applications.
4. Grant Accessibility, Automation, and Screen Recording permissions when prompted.
5. Install the Aether iOS app on your iPhone.
6. Open the iOS app, tap **Pair**, and scan the QR code shown in the Mac menu bar.

Your iPhone and Mac must be on the same network during pairing.

---

## What you can do

- Open, focus, and quit Mac apps by name
- Run multi-step workflows from a single sentence
- Type text, trigger keyboard shortcuts, take screenshots
- Voice mode for hands-free control
- Pair multiple Macs and switch between them

---

## Requirements

- macOS 13 or later (Apple Silicon recommended)
- iPhone running iOS 17 or later
- Both devices on the same Wi-Fi network during pairing

---

## Troubleshooting

- **"Aether can't be opened because Apple cannot check it for malicious software"** — right-click the app and choose **Open**, then confirm.
- **Pairing fails** — make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network and that Aether is running on your Mac.
- **Commands don't execute** — open System Settings → Privacy & Security and confirm Aether has Accessibility, Automation, and Screen Recording permissions.

---

## Privacy

- No accounts, no sign-up required.
- Aether does not collect, store, or share personal data.
- Voice transcription on iPhone happens on-device.
